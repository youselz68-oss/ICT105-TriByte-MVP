## Team Working Agreement
1. **Meeting schedule:** Meet after class when needed. If no meeting is needed, give updates on LINE instead.
2. **Communication channel:** LINE group for quick updates. Reply within 24 hours.
3. **Task management:** Each member picks up tasks based on their role. If someone is busy, let the team know early so others can help.
4. **File naming rule:** Use lowercase-with-hyphen for all files, for example `idea-log.md`
5. **GitHub commit rule:** Commit after every completed task with a short clear message describing what was done.
6. **Deadlines:** Agree on deadlines together during the meeting. If someone cannot finish on time, inform the team at least one day before.
7. **Conflict resolution:** Discuss openly in the group chat or meeting. If no agreement is reached, the team votes and the majority decides.
8. **Respect rule:** Be honest, be kind, and support each other. No one is left to do everything alone.
